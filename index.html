Kaktag invests in early-stage founders and tech innovators driven by passion. We back those who tackle real-world challenges and dream of making a positive impact on their communities, nation and beyond.

Approach

Kaktag was created by founders who understand how tough it can be to turn a dream into reality, from the first idea to launching and scaling. When we invest in you, we’re with you every step of the way—like a good friend, helping and guiding you. We always put your interests as a founder first. We are committed to helping you understand and adapt to market changes, timing, and trends in a way that benefits everyone.
